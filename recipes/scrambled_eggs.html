Scrambled Eggs

About scrambled eggs:

Scrambled eggs are a classic breakfast dish that is simple to make and can be customized to your liking.

They are made by whisking eggs together with milk or cream, salt, and pepper.

The egg mixture is then cooked in a skillet over low heat, stirring gently until the eggs are set but still slightly runny.

Scrambled eggs are a versatile dish that can be enjoyed on their own or topped with your favorite ingredients, such as cheese, avocado, herbs, or hot sauce.

They are also a good source of protein and healthy fats.

Ingredients:

  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tablespoon milk or cream
  • Pinch of salt and pepper
  • 1 tablespoon unsalted butter

Steps:

  1. Crack the eggs into a bowl and whisk together until well combined.
  2. Add the milk, salt, and pepper and whisk again.
  3. Melt the butter in a nonstick skillet over medium-low heat.
  4. Pour the egg mixture into the skillet and let it sit for 1-2 minutes without stirring.
  5. Using a spatula, gently fold the cooked edges of the eggs towards the center of the pan.
  6. Continue folding until the eggs are almost set but still slightly runny.
  7. Remove the pan from the heat and let the eggs rest for 1-2 minutes.
  8. Serve immediately with your favorite toppings.
